# JETT Tokenomics Overview

The JETT token is designed to be the native utility and governance token of the Jett Casino ecosystem, built on the Solana blockchain. Its primary purpose is to drive platform engagement, incentivize player loyalty, and enable community participation in the platform's success.

## Token Utility

The JETT token will have several key utility functions within the Jett Casino ecosystem:

1.  **In-Game Credits/Wagering**: While initial gameplay will use stablecoins, JETT will serve as an optional in-game currency, offering players potential benefits like reduced house edge or exclusive game access. This creates a direct demand for the token within the gaming loop.
2.  **Staking for House Edge Share**: JETT token holders will have the opportunity to stake their tokens to receive a portion of the platform's house edge revenue (in the form of USDC or SOL). This mechanism aligns token holder interests with the success of the casino, creating a sustainable income stream for loyal community members.
3.  **Governance**: Staked JETT tokens will grant holders voting rights on key platform decisions, such as:
    *   New game development priorities.
    *   RTP adjustments (within regulatory limits).
    *   Marketing and community fund allocation.
    *   Future platform features and partnerships.
    *   JETT tokenomics adjustments.
4.  **Exclusive Access & Rewards**: JETT holders may gain access to VIP tournaments, exclusive games, higher withdrawal limits, or boosted loyalty rewards.

## Token Distribution

The total supply of JETT tokens will be fixed to ensure scarcity and value. The proposed distribution is as follows:

*   **Players & Rewards (40%)**: Allocated for in-game rewards, airdrops, loyalty programs, staking rewards, and player acquisition incentives. This ensures a broad distribution to the active user base.
*   **Treasury (25%)**: Reserved for future ecosystem development, operational costs, liquidity provisioning, and strategic partnerships. Governed by the DAO.
*   **Team (20%)**: Allocated to the founding team and core contributors, vested over 3-4 years with a 6-12 month cliff to ensure long-term commitment.
*   **Advisors & Partners (15%)**: Allocated to strategic advisors, early investors, and key ecosystem partners, vested over 2-3 years with a 6-month cliff.

## Vesting Schedule

*   **Team Tokens**: 12-month cliff, followed by linear vesting over 36 months.
*   **Advisor/Partner Tokens**: 6-month cliff, followed by linear vesting over 24 months.
*   **Player & Treasury Tokens**: Released strategically based on roadmap milestones, community growth, and staking demand, managed by the DAO.

## Why Solana?

Jett Casino chose Solana for its robust technical advantages:

*   **Speed**: Solana's high transaction throughput (TPS) ensures a seamless, lag-free gaming experience, critical for real-time interactive games.
*   **Cost**: Negligible transaction fees make micro-transactions viable for in-game actions, benefiting both players and the platform.
*   **VRF Integration**: Solana's Verifiable Random Function (VRF) capabilities provide a transparent, tamper-proof source of randomness, enhancing the provable fairness of all games.
*   **Scalability**: Solana's architecture can support millions of users, ensuring Jett Casino can scale without performance degradation as its user base grows.

## Beta Phase & Mainnet Launch Timeline

During the beta phase, Jett Casino will operate on Solana Devnet, utilizing simulated JETT tokens for testing game mechanics, smart contract interactions, and user experience without real financial risk.

The full JETT token mainnet launch will be directly tied to securing pre-seed funding and achieving critical roadmap milestones, including:
*   Successful Solana mainnet integration for core game mechanics.
*   Acquisition of the Anjouan B2C gaming license.
*   Establishment of a significant user base during the beta period.

The mainnet launch is anticipated in Q3 2026, coinciding with the platform's official regulatory approval and full operational readiness.
