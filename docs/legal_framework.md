# Legal Framework: Interactive Risk Management Gaming

Jett Casino operates under a novel classification we term "Interactive Risk Management Gaming." ## Legal Classification Rationale
The primary rationale for "Interactive Risk Management Gaming" is the demonstrable impact of player action on the probability and magnitude of wins. Unlike slot machines or roulette, where player input is minimal or non-existent beyond placing a bet, Jett Casino's games demand active engagement, strategic choices, and precise execution. This moves them closer to categories like esports, arcade redemption games, or even strategic card games, where skill is acknowledged as a dominant factor.
